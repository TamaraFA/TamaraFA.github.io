About

The Neuroscience & AI Workshop 2025 brings together experts from cognitive science, machine learning, and computational neuroscience to discuss shared challenges, emerging theories, and transformative applications at the intersection of brain research and AI.

Key Topics

Brain-Inspired Models

Neuromorphic computing, spiking neural networks, and learning mechanisms.

AI for Neuroscience

Advanced ML techniques for brain imaging and behavioral analysis.

Cognitive Architectures

Bridging human cognition and artificial intelligence.
