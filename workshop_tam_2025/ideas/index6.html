Workshop on Applied Mathematics 2025

June 10–11, 2025 • University of Cambridge

About Topics Speakers Program Registration

About

This workshop brings together researchers, graduate students, and industry practitioners to discuss current advances and applications in applied mathematics. The focus will be on bridging theory and practice through interdisciplinary collaboration.

Topics of Interest

  • Numerical Analysis
  • Optimization Techniques
  • Mathematical Modeling in the Sciences
  • Applied Probability and Statistics
  • Computational Methods

Invited Speakers

  • Prof. Anna Müller (ETH Zürich)
  • Dr. Robert Johnson (MIT)
  • Prof. Li Wei (Tsinghua University)

Program Overview

The workshop will feature keynote talks, contributed paper sessions, and a panel discussion on future directions in applied mathematics. A detailed schedule will be available by May 1, 2025.

Registration

Registration opens on March 15, 2025. Early bird deadline: May 10, 2025. Please visit the registration portal for details.
