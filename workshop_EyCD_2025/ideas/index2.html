About the Workshop

The Quantum Computing Workshop 2025 brings together researchers and practitioners to explore the latest developments in quantum algorithms, hardware, and cross-disciplinary applications. This two-day event fosters collaboration between academia and industry in the emerging field of quantum technology.

Workshop Topics

Quantum Algorithms

Discover cutting-edge research on optimization, cryptography, and simulation.

Hardware Advances

Learn about the latest breakthroughs in qubits, error correction, and scalability.

Industry Applications

Real-world use cases in finance, chemistry, and machine learning.
