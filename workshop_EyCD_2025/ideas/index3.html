About the Workshop

This workshop gathers statisticians, data scientists, and researchers to discuss advances in methodology, applications, and interdisciplinary collaboration. Attendees will engage with tutorials, talks, and discussions to deepen their knowledge in modern statistical practice.

Workshop Topics

Bayesian Methods

Applications and new developments in Bayesian modeling and computation.

Machine Learning

Statistical perspectives on predictive modeling and algorithm evaluation.

Data Visualization

Techniques to explore and communicate insights from complex datasets.
